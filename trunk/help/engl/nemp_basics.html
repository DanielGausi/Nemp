Basics

The basic concept of Nem p is as follows:
  1. There is one playlist. Not two ore three, only one. The items in this playlist will be played one after the other, or randomly.
  2. The Media Library manages all your audio files. You can browse in the library, or search for a specific title.
The philosophy behind Nemp is KISS - Keep it simple, stupid!

New features will be implemented, if (and only if) the basic handling won't become more confusing ore complicated by them.

The nemp window consists of four parts.



1. The Player
The center part is the actual player. On the top you will find the usual control and display elements. Below that part some addtional information is shown (like cover art or lyrics), or additional controls (like equalizer, effects, headphones).
Tip: Just click the big Play-Button to start playing some files.

2. The Playlist
Display of the current playlist. click on the button for the playlist menu.
Tip: Try Drag&Drop to fill the playlist.

3. Media Library, overview
The media library manages your audio files. You can browse your library by three different ways:
Classic view. The library is sorted by the selected criterias, e.g. Artist and Album.
Coverflow. The albums in your library are shown in a coverflow.
Tagcloud. All the tags in your library are organized in a tagcloud.

Tip: Drag&Drop your "Music folder" here to start building your library.

4. Media Libray, title list
The bottom part of the Nemp window shows all titles of your library, that matches the current selection in the overview part (3), or - after using the quicksearch - the current search results.
Tip: Try Drag&Drop from here to the playlist. Or drag some files to the Windows Explorer to copy some files.
