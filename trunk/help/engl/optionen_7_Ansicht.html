Party mode

A few years ago somebody mailed me, that Nemp is a wonderful player for parties, as everybody can understand the GUI very fast. But the buttons are getting smaller and smaller after a few beers and become harder to hit...

So, in party mode the buttons are larger, and some features are blocked (e.g. to avoid deleting the library).

Tip: Lock the Nemp-PC on a party, and offer your guests access to the player by the Nemp webserver. For a detailed manual including setup of a local wifi network, please visit www.gausi.de
