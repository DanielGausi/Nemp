Scrobble with Nemp

To Scrobble is a concept of LastFM - a social network with focus on music. Scrobling means, to share the information what you are currently listening to with your friends. LastFM will analyse your taste of music and recommends other users with a similar one.

You can scrobble with Nemp.


Configure Nemp for scrobbling
At first Nemp needs access to your user profile on LastFM: Choose Settings -> LastFM and click the Button "Start" and follow the instructions.

Activate scrobbling
Within teh settings dialog you can choose to scrobble just the current session (scrobble until you close Nemp), or to scrobble always. Scrobbling will start after click on the "Ok" button.
When Nemp is scrobbling your playlist, there will be an icon in the main window. You can stop scrobbling by a click on this icon.

What does Nemp do while scrobbling?
Scrobbling consists of to stages.
  • The "Now-Playing"-message. Every time a new track starts, nemp will notify LastFM. On your LastFM profile will appear something like "currently listens to <current track>".
  • The "Submission"-Message. This notification is sent at the end of a track. Note that Nemp will only "submit" the track, if at least 50% (or 4 minutes, whatever occurs first) of the track has been played. Short tracks (less than 30 seconds) are completely  ignored. After a submit the track will appear in your history on LastFM.
Troubleshooting
Sometimes scrobbling doesn't work properly. Nemp will stop scrobbling in these cases. Possible error sources are
  • No internet connection
  • A firewall is blocking Nemp
  • The LastFM session key is invalid. You should restart the configuration to get a new session key.
  • Your account was blocked. You should contact LastFM for further assistance.
When you think, that you have fixed the source of error, you can click on "Scrobble again" in the settings dialog to try again..
