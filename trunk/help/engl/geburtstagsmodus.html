Birthday mode

The Birthday Mode is a little gimmick included in Nemp. Set a time, when you want to play a specific song. Optionally you can choose a "Countdown song", which will be played just before the "Birthday song".

When the Birthday Mode is activated, the Symbol will be displayed in the main window.

This may be used, for playing a "Happy birthday" at midnight, whe you have a party on the eve of your birthday.

Afterwards Nemp will proceed with the playlist.
