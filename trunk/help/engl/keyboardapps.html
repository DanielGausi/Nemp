Keyboard-Display

Nemp supports the Logitech G15 Keyboard. You can control the playback through the 4 buttons at the display.



This is not integrated in the main .exe-file, but is realized by an additional program, which communicates with Nemp through the Nemp API.

Tip: If you have another type of keyboard with display, you may write a proper tool for your model. If you have some experience in software developing, it shouldn't be very hard. Feel free to ask for further instructions how to do it.
