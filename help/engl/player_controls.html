Display / Controls of the player



Title display
Tip: Switch between display modes by clicking the title:
  • Display of "Artist - Title" (as it appears in the playlist)
  • Display of the filename, including the complete path
  • Additional information like bitrate, samplerate, duration
  • Lyrics (if existing)
Rating (stars)
Tip: Rate the current title with a single click.

Display of activated tools (not visible in the image above)
If you use some of the Nemp tools, a matching icon will be shown below the rating stars.
The countdown to shutdown is active. What kind of shutdown (close Nemp, suspend, hibernate, shutdown) has been selected is shown in the hint text (point on the icon for a second or two). 
The Birthday mode is active. Nemp will suspend the playlist at the given time for a "Happy Birthday".
The Nemp webserver is active to grant access to the media library and the player through a webbrowser (e.g. choose the next track to play through your smartphone)
Scrobbling is active. The songs you are currently listening to are shared with your friends on last.fm.
The walkman mode is active, and the battery status is low. Nemp flutters the playback.

Time display
Tip: Switch between "time" and "time remaining" by clicking.

Volume control
Click&Drag the vertical slider to change the volume of the plyback

Progress control
Click&Drag the horizontal slider.

Control of the player

Tip: Some buttons have more than one function. Try the right mouse button for context menus!

Playback order



The four symbols indicate
  • Repeat everything. Nemp will play every title in the playlist and repeat from the beginning after the last one.
  • Repeat current track: Only the current track is repeated in a loop.
  • Random mode: The tracks in the playlist a played randomly. Note: In the extended playlist settings you can adjust between "really random" (which means that a song can be played twice within a short time) or "Avoid repetitions" (which means if a song was played, it is blocked for some time).
  • Repeat off: Nemp will play every title in the playlist and stop after the last one.
Recording

The record button is only accessible on mp3-webstreams.

Recording not possible (either you are playing a local file, or the codec is not supported for recording).
Recording is possible. Click the button to begin with recording the current webstream.
Nemp is recording the current stream. Click the button to stop.

The recording is saved into the directory given in the webradio settings. You can open it through the menu "Tools -> Directories -> Recordings".
